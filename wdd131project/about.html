About the Photographer

The story behind the lens

Pius Mukisa

Travel Photographer & Visual Storyteller

My journey as a photographer began over a decade ago when I left my corporate job with nothing but a backpack and an entry-level DSLR camera. What started as a six-month sabbatical turned into a lifelong passion for capturing the world's most breathtaking moments.

Based in Uganda, Kampala but rarely at home, I've had the privilege of documenting landscapes, cultures, and wildlife across all seven continents. My work focuses on finding the extraordinary in everyday scenes and revealing the connection between humans and their environment.

When I'm not behind the camera, I lead photography workshops and contribute to conservation efforts worldwide. I believe that powerful imagery can inspire protection of our planet's most vulnerable places and communities.

Philosophy

I approach each scene with patience, seeking the perfect moment when light, composition, and subject come together. Rather than heavy post-processing, I focus on getting it right in camera.

Editing Process

My post-processing workflow emphasizes natural colors and contrast while preserving the authentic atmosphere of each location. I use Adobe Lightroom for most adjustments and Photoshop for finishing touches.
